16-bit pixel art items and projectiles sprite sheet for Zelda-like RPG.
Style: Classic SNES top-down.

Items Required:
1. **Bomb**:
   - Idle on ground (round black bomb with fuse)
   - Fuse burning animation (3 frames)

2. **Boomerang**:
   - Spinning animation (4 angles/frames)
3. **Arrow**:
   - Up, Down, Left, Right facing
4. **Heart pickup**:
   - Full heart (bouncing/floating)

Output:
- Transparent background
- Organized rows
- Distinct frame separation
